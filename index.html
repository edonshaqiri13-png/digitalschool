What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is when computers are taught to think and learn like humans. Instead of just following strict instructions, AI can look at examples, find patterns, and make decisions on its own.

AI learns from data – lots of examples like pictures, numbers, or words. By studying these examples, it figures out patterns and uses them to make predictions or solve new problems it hasn't seen before.

AI in Your Daily Life

📺

YouTube Recommendations

YouTube looks at the videos you've watched, liked, and how long you watched them. It finds patterns in your viewing habits to suggest videos you might like next.

📱

Face Unlock

Your phone's camera creates a detailed map of your face by analyzing thousands of tiny details. When you look at your phone, it compares this live scan to your stored facial data to unlock securely.

🦉

Duolingo Lessons

Duolingo tracks which words and grammar concepts you struggle with or excel at. It then adjusts the difficulty and content of future lessons to match your personal learning pace.
